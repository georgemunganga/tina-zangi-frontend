 # Zangi Website – Content Tone

Define the tone of voice for the Zangi website.

## Tone Should Feel

- warm
- adventurous
- imaginative
- inspiring
- culturally rich
- confident

## Audience

Children who want adventure  
Parents who want meaningful learning.

## Define

- brand voice principles
- headline style
- product description tone
- parent messaging tone
- vocabulary style

Avoid:

- overly childish language
- corporate jargon
- generic marketing tone
